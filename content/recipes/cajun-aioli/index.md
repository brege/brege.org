**Author:** Wyatt Brege



### Ingredients

-   1/2 Gallon Mayonaise
-   3 Tbsp Cajun Seasoning
-   1/4 Cup Lemon Juice
-   1/3 Cup Roasted Red Pepper Sauce

### Instructions 

In a large mixing bowl, whisk ingredients together.
