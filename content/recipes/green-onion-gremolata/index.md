**Author:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 1 Quart


### Ingredients

-   2 bunch Green Onion
-   1 bunch Parsley
-   1 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
-   1/4 Cup Chopped Garlic
-   4 each Lemons (zest and juice)
-   tt Salt and Fine Black Pepper

### Instructions 

Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
