## Green Goddess

**Author:** Matt Andrews, Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 1 quarts


### Ingredients:

-   1 Quart Mayonaisse
-   1 Bunch Chive
-   1 Bunch Tarragon
-   1 Bunch Parsley
-   tt Salt and Pepper

### Directions: 

Add all ingredients to a 4 Quart Container.
Blend with immersion blender until the mayonnaise turns green.
Season.
