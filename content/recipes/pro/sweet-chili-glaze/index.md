## Sweet Chili Glaze

**Chefs:** Wyatt Brege, Ryan Fleener



### Ingredients:

-   2 Bottles Mae Ploy Sweet Chilli Sauce
-   2 Tbs Honey
-   2 Cup Orange Juice

### Directions: 

Whisk ingredients in a large saute pan.
Place pan on low heat and reduce sauce by a third.
