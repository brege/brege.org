## BBQ Sauce (midwest style)

**Author:** Ginger Brege, Wyatt Brege



### Ingredients:

-   1 can Ketchup
-   2 Cup Yellow Mustard
-   3 Cup Brown Sugar
-   1/2 Cup Worcestershire Sauce

### Directions: 

Mix in a mixing bowl with a whisk until blended and smooth.
