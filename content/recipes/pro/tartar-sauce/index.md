## Tartar Sauce

**Chef:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 6 Quarts

### Ingredients:

- 1 Gallon Mayonnaise
- 2 Cup Relish
- 2 fl oz Worcestershire Sauce
- 4 fl oz Pickle Juice
- 2 oz Red Onion, small dice
- 2 fl oz Sriracha
- 4 fl oz Lemon Juice
- 4 oz Honey
- 1 Tbsp Kosher Salt
- 1 Tbsp Black Pepper

### Directions:

1.  Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl with a whisk
