## Fried Chicken Dredge

**Chef:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 2 Quarts


### Ingredients:

-   2 Quarts All-Purpose Flour
-   4 Tbsp Kosher Salt
-   3 Tbsp Onion Powder
-   2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
-   1 Tbsp Fine Black Pepper
-   1 bunch Majoram

### Directions: 

Pick the majoram, then combine all ingredients in a medium sized mixing
bowl.
