## Thousand Island / Awesome Sauce

**Chef:** Wyatt Brege



### Ingredients:

-   1 1/2 Quart Mayonaisse
-   2 Cup Ketchup
-   1 Cup Yellow Mustard
-   1 Cup House Pickle Juice

### Directions: 

In a medium mixing bowl combine all ingredients until smooth.
