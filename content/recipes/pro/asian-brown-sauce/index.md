## Asian Brown Sauce

**Author:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 1 Quart


### Ingredients:

-   1 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
-   1/2 Cup Water
-   3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
-   1/4 Cup Sriracha
-   1/3 Cup Oyster Sauce
-   18 ea Garlic Cloves
-   1/3 Cup Fresh Ginger
-   1/4 Cup Sesame Oil
-   4 ea Plums, pits removed

### Directions: 

Peel and grate the ginger.

In a 4 Quart container, add all ingredients and blend with the immersion
blender.
