### Garlic Aioli

**Author:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 2 Quart


#### Ingredients:

-   7 Cup Mayonaisse
-   1/2 Cup Garlic Puree
-   tt Salt and Restaurant Ground Black Pepper

#### Directions: 

In the Ninja blender attachment, add all ingredients and use the
\"Smoothie\" setting to full cycle.
