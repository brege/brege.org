### Coleslaw Dressing

**Author:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 1 Quart 

#### Ingredients:

- 1 ½ Cup Mayonnaise
- 1 Cup Dijon Mustard
- 1 Cup Honey
- ¼ Cup Pickle Juice
- ¼ Cup White Wine Vinegar
- 2 Tbsp Onion Powder
- 1 ½ Tbsp Black Pepper
- 1 ½ Tbsp Kosher Salt

#### Directions:

1.  Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl with a whisk

2.  Combine the sauce with approximately 5 lb shredded green cabbage and
    2.5 lb shredded red cabbage then mix
