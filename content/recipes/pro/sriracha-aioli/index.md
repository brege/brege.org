### Sriracha Aioli

**Author:** Wyatt Brege



#### Ingredients:

-   3 Quart Mayonaise
-   14 fl oz Sriracha
-   1/3 Cup Lemon Juice

#### Directions: 

In a large mixing bowl add all ingredients and whisk until blended
smooth.
