## French Toast Batter

**Chef:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 2 quarts


### Ingredients:

-   1 Quart Liquid Egg Mix
-   1 Quart Heavy Cream
-   3 Tbsp Cinnamon
-   1/8 tsp Nutmeg
-   1 tsp Vanilla Extract

### Directions: 

In a large mixing bowl, whisk all ingredients together.
