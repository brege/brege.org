**Author:** Wyatt Brege

**Yield:** 5 Quarts


### Ingredients

-   1 Gallon Mayonnaise
-   17 fl oz Sriracha
-   1/2 Cup Lemon Juice

### Instructions 

In a large mixing bowl add all ingredients and whisk until blended
smooth.
